MyMcq - උසස් පෙළ

Sinhala medium mcq papers for students who are doing A/L examination to improve knowledge while managing given time precisely. Students can find mcq papers in different categories like past papers, quiz papers, and time-based exam papers.

Live Link

Project Overview

For the implementation of this application, I have used Flutter, Node.js, and MySQL. Advanced Level students can use this application to do model papers and quizzes for their subjects. Subjects categorize according to streams. Initially, the student should register to the system by inserting the requested information. Successful registration will direct the student to the inside of the application. Students can do model papers and quizzes according to the preferred subject available on the home page. When starting the question paper, the countdown will begin to count the time. When time is up, the question paper will submit automatically, and display achieved marks. Otherwise, the student can manually submit when it is required to stop, and view achieved results. The student can view the answer sheet for the submitted question paper. Also, the answer sheet can download as a pdf file if the student is needed to download it.

Students can also find exam papers that are conducting by teachers. They are time-based papers. Before expiring the availability of question paper, the student should complete it. After the deadline, students can find achieved results and rank for completed question papers. These are available on the page, Exams. On the page, Leaderboard, students can find their district rank according to each subject. To implement this ranking system, I have used the total points of each student for each subject. Then it is categorized under their districts. Students can view this dashboard according to the year they are going to sit for the A/L examination. Students cannot view other students' details who are going to do the exam in another year. The student can find his total points, district rank, and island rank for each subject on the Achievement page. Students can view and edit their details, such as profile picture, name, and password, from the Profile page.

Also, teachers can use this application to conduct their exams. Registered teachers can view added papers and check papers. After the deadline of added question papers, teachers can find scores of participated students. Teachers can add their advertisements through this system to display along with their question papers. Teachers also can edit profile details.

Admin panel is using to manage activities in the system. Functions of the admin panel are adding necessary details, removing unnecessary information, inserting, editing, and removing question papers, questions, and answers. Sometimes it is required to have equations in Advanced level papers. Tex syntaxes are used for adding equations with the use of necessary libraries. Admin can view how questions are displaying before inserting them into the database.
